5 Bed New Build

Modern Living Redefined: Stunning 5-Bedroom New Build Design

Our team worked on an archviz project to design and landscape a new 5 bedroom house build. The client approached us with a desire for a modern and spacious home that emphasized indoor-outdoor living. We collaborated closely with the client to develop a comprehensive brief that included their specific requirements for room layouts, finishes, lighting design, and landscaping features.

Our team created highly detailed 3D models of the proposed design, which accurately represented the client's vision. We incorporated a range of natural lighting scenarios, demonstrating how the space would look throughout the day. Our designers also created a range of attractive landscaping features, including hardscaping, decking, native plants, and water features.

To provide an immersive experience for the client, we created a series of animations that provided a virtual tour of the property, showcasing how the various elements of the design would work together. This allowed the client to fully experience the space and make informed decisions about their vision.

The final product was a beautiful and functional space that the client was extremely happy with. The visualization helped them to communicate their vision to contractors and other stakeholders. Our team successfully created a stunning design that seamlessly blended with the natural surroundings and provided a perfect environment for indoor-outdoor living.
