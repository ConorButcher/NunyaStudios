Lambourne Jockey Club

Redefining Equestrian Excellence: A Stunning Archviz Portfolio for a Prestigious Jockey Club

The project was for a prestigious jockey club located in a beautiful rural setting. The club had recently undergone a major renovation, and the client wanted us to create a series of visualizations that showcased the updated facilities and the surrounding landscape.

Our team started by conducting a site visit to get a sense of the existing conditions and to gather information about the project requirements. We met with the client to discuss their vision for the space, and we also reviewed the architectural plans and landscaping drawings.

Once we had a good understanding of the project scope, we began developing a series of 3D models that accurately reflected the proposed changes to the facilities and landscape. We used a combination of CAD and 3D modeling software to create detailed digital models of the site and the buildings.

Our team then began the process of adding textures, lighting, and other details to the models to create realistic and immersive visualizations. We created a variety of renderings from different angles to give the client a complete picture of how the space would look.

We paid special attention to the landscaping, as the natural surroundings were a key feature of the property. We used a combination of native plants and carefully selected ornamental species to create a lush and inviting environment around the buildings and along the pathways.

In addition to the visualizations, we also created a series of animations that provided a virtual tour of the property. This allowed the client to experience the space in a more immersive way and get a better sense of how the various elements of the design fit together.

Overall, the project was a great success, and the client was extremely happy with the results. Our visualizations helped them to better understand the proposed changes and to communicate their vision to stakeholders and investors. The final product was a beautiful and functional space that blended seamlessly with the natural surroundings and provided a world-class experience for the jockeys and their horses.
