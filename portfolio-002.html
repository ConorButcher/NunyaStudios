Bungalow Conversion

Reviving a Classic: A Stunning Vision for a Complete Bungalow Conversion

Our client had recently purchased an older bungalow and was looking to completely renovate and modernize the interior and exterior of the property. They approached us to create a series of visualizations that would showcase the proposed changes and give them a better idea of how the finished product would look.

Our team started by conducting a site visit to get a sense of the existing conditions and to gather information about the client's vision for the space. We then began developing a series of 3D models that accurately reflected the proposed changes, including new fixtures, finishes, and layout modifications.

We worked closely with the client to ensure that the design reflected their style and preferences, and we incorporated a range of modern and stylish features to give the property a fresh and contemporary look. We paid particular attention to the lighting design, using a combination of natural and artificial light to create a warm and inviting atmosphere.

In addition to the interior design, we also created a series of visualizations that showcased the updated exterior of the property. This included new landscaping features, as well as modifications to the facade to create a more modern and attractive look.

Overall, the project was a great success, and the client was extremely pleased with the results. Our visualizations helped them to better understand the proposed changes and to communicate their vision to contractors and other stakeholders. The final product was a beautiful and functional space that reflected the client's style and provided a comfortable and modern living environment.
